Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026 · Effective immediately

🇪🇺 FixMyElo is GDPR compliant. We are based in Cyprus, a member of the European Union. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what we absolutely need.

1. Who We Are

FixMyElo ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered League of Legends coaching platform operated from Cyprus, European Union. Our website is fixmyelo.com. You can contact us at privacy@fixmyelo.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use FixMyElo:

  • Summoner Name & Region — to fetch your game data from Riot Games API
  • Email Address — when you sign up for Pro or book a coaching session
  • Payment Information — processed securely by Stripe (we never store card details)
  • Usage Data — pages visited, features used (anonymous analytics only)
  • Coach Application Data — if you apply to be a coach: summoner name, rank, Discord, email

We do NOT collect passwords, personal ID documents, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate your AI coaching report via the Riot Games API
  • To process payments for reports and coaching sessions
  • To connect players with coaches for booked sessions
  • To send you your report and booking confirmations via email
  • To improve our AI analysis and platform features

4. Riot Games API

We use the official Riot Games API to fetch your League of Legends match history. This access is read-only — we never log into your account, never modify your account, and never store your login credentials. Your Summoner Name is used only to retrieve publicly available match data from Riot's servers.

5. Third Party Services

  • Riot Games API — for game data (subject to Riot's Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — for payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI analysis (data is not stored by Anthropic beyond the session)
  • Vercel — for website hosting

6. Your GDPR Rights

As an EU resident you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object — object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@fixmyelo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Report data is retained for 90 days. Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by EU law. You may request deletion at any time.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies strictly necessary for the platform to function (session management, payment processing). We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data to third parties. Ever.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Payment data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never touches our servers. We conduct regular security reviews.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy questions or to exercise your rights: privacy@fixmyelo.com
