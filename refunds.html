1. AI Reports (€5 Full Report)

Because AI reports are digital products delivered instantly upon payment, we generally do not offer refunds. However we will issue a full refund if:

  • The report failed to generate due to a technical error on our end
  • The Riot API was unavailable and we could not retrieve your data
  • You were charged but received no report

We will NOT refund reports because:

  • You disagree with the AI's analysis
  • You entered the wrong summoner name (but we'll give you one free redo!)
  • You changed your mind after receiving the report

This is in line with EU Digital Content Directive — digital goods delivered immediately are exempt from the standard 14-day withdrawal right once delivery has begun with your consent.

2. Coaching Sessions

Full refund if:

  • Your coach does not show up for the booked session
  • You cancel at least 24 hours before the session start time
  • The coach cancels the session for any reason

50% refund if:

  • You cancel less than 24 hours before the session

No refund if:

  • The session was completed as booked
  • You did not show up without cancelling

3. Pro Monthly Subscription (€15/month)

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future billing immediately. We do not refund the current billing period. Your Pro access continues until the end of the paid period.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@fixmyelo.com with:

  • Your email address used for payment
  • Your Summoner Name
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Your Stripe payment receipt number (in your confirmation email)

We process all refund requests within 3 business days. Refunds appear on your statement within 5-10 business days depending on your bank.

5. EU Consumer Rights

As an EU-based company we comply fully with EU consumer protection law including the Consumer Rights Directive. If you have a complaint that we cannot resolve, you have the right to use the EU Online Dispute Resolution platform at ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr.

6. Contact

Refund questions? Email support@fixmyelo.com — we reply within 24 hours. 😊
